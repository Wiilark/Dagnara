Features

Everything you need to crush your goals

From AI food recognition to personalized macro targets — Dagnara has the tools serious nutrition tracking demands.

📸
AI Photo Logging

Snap a photo of any meal and our AI instantly identifies every food item and calculates accurate macros — no barcode scanning needed.

🎯
Smart Macro Targets

Your TDEE is calculated from body stats and activity level. Protein, carbs, and fat targets adapt to your weight goal — cut, maintain, or bulk.

📊
Progress Tracking

Visual weight history, XP-based streaks, and weekly trends keep you motivated and show exactly how far you've come.

🍽️
Recipe Import

Paste any recipe URL and Dagnara automatically extracts ingredients, calculates per-serving macros, and saves it to your library.

💪
Training Programs

Follow structured workout programs synced to your nutrition goals — fuel your training with the right macros at the right time.

Smart Reminders

Customizable meal reminders, hydration nudges, and check-ins keep you consistent without being annoying.
