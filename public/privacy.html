Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Dagnara ("we", "us", "the app") is a nutrition and wellness tracking application. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We collect the minimum needed to run the app and never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Profile & goals — information you enter such as age, sex, height, weight, target weight, activity level, and dietary preferences, used to calculate your calorie and macro targets.
  • Nutrition & activity logs — the foods, water, weight, mood, sleep, steps, and workouts you record in your diary.
  • Food photos — when you use AI food analysis, the photo is sent to our server and to our AI provider (Anthropic) solely to identify the food and estimate nutrition. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Health data (optional) — if you grant permission, the app reads steps, active calories, and sleep from Apple Health or Android Health Connect. This data is read on your device to display your activity and is not sold or shared.
  • Subscription data — if you purchase Premium, payment is processed by Stripe. We store only your subscription status and Stripe customer identifier, never your full card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: tracking, calorie/macro goals, progress, and AI food estimation.
  • To sync your data across your devices.
  • To send notifications you have enabled (meal reminders, streaks, check-ins).
  • To manage Premium subscriptions and prevent abuse of our AI services.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your account and diary data are stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security, so each account can only access its own data. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. A copy is also cached on your device so the app works offline.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting.
  • Anthropic — AI food photo analysis, text nutrition estimation, and coaching. Inputs are processed to generate a response and are not used to identify you.
  • Open Food Facts — barcode and food-name lookups (public food database).
  • Stripe — payment processing for Premium subscriptions.
  • Apple Health / Android Health Connect — optional, on-device activity data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the service providers above, strictly to operate the app, and only to the extent each service requires.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & export — your data is visible in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Delete Account inside the app. This removes your profile, diary, and app state from our servers.
  • Permissions — camera, photos, health access, and notifications are all optional and can be revoked in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Dagnara is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email privacy@dagnara.com.
